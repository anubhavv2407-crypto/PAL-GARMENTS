Our Story

Seven Decades of Stitching Trust, Quality & Legacy

Founded in 1956, Pal Garments began with a single promise — to provide the highest quality school uniforms to the children of Gonda. What started as a vision has grown into a full-scale operation trusted by generations.

We operate under our in-house brand Palji, giving us complete control over every step — from fabric selection to final stitching. No middlemen, no compromise.
